Dear Hiring Manager,

I am a driven student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Management Information Systems with a minor in Software Systems at UNC Charlotte. I’m enthusiastic about contributing to Bank of America’s innovative approach to technology and data strategy. I would welcome the opportunity to speak with you further about how I can support your team’s goals. Please feel free to reach out at calebrobertgreen@gmail.com.

Thank you for your time and consideration. I look forward to connecting soon.
